Privacy Policy – Body Metrics

Last updated: June 2026

Body Metrics ("the app") is developed by MrNick Apps. This privacy policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

1. Data We Collect

The app collects the following data that you enter manually:

  • Body measurements (weight, biceps, arm, chest, waist, hips, thigh, calf)
  • Dates associated with each measurement

No name, email address, phone number, or other personally identifiable information is collected.

2. Authentication

The app uses anonymous authentication via Firebase Authentication (Google). You are assigned a random, anonymous user ID when you first open the app. No account creation or login is required.

3. Data Storage

Your measurement data is stored securely in Google Firebase Firestore, associated only with your anonymous user ID. Data is stored in the European Union (europe-west1).

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or disclose your data to any third parties, except as required by the infrastructure services listed below.

5. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication & Firestore) – Privacy Policy

6. Data Deletion

You can delete individual measurements at any time by long-pressing an entry within the app. To request complete deletion of all your data, contact us at the email below and we will remove your data from Firebase within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Body Metrics is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
